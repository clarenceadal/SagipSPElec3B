# SAGIP

## Project Overview

SAGIP is a campus emergency response and incident management platform designed for the University of San Jose-Recoletos (USJ-R). The system aims to improve emergency reporting and response coordination by allowing students to quickly send emergency alerts to the Safety and Security Department (SSD) together with their location and incident details.

---

## Problem Statement

Emergency incidents are often reported late due to:

- Delayed communication
- Unclear incident locations
- Lack of centralized reporting
- Incomplete emergency information

SAGIP addresses these issues by providing a centralized emergency reporting platform.

---

## Objectives

- Enable students to submit emergency reports quickly.
- Provide SSD personnel with real-time incident information.
- Improve emergency response coordination.
- Maintain records of reported incidents.
- Facilitate campus-wide emergency broadcasts.

---
